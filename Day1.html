Word: While

TopicDetails
WordWhile
Tamil Meaningபோது / அதே நேரத்தில் / வரை / ஆனால் (contrast)
Pronunciation (Tamil)வாயில் (va-il)
Why we use? 2 actions same time-la நடக்குது, duration-க்காக, அல்லது contrast-க்காக use பண்ணுவோம்
Example:
While eating, I listen to music → சாப்பிடும் போது, நான் music கேட்கிறேன்
Why we use?Example Sentence
2 actions same time-la நடக்குது (Simultaneous)While I was studying, my friend called me
Duration / கொஞ்ச நேரம் வரைWait for a while → கொஞ்ச நேரம் வரை காத்திரு
Contrast / ஆனால்He is rich while I am poor → அவன் பணக்காரன் ஆனால் நான் இல்லை

Word: While – Usage Overview

Context Meaning
Two actions happening together அதே நேரத்தில் (Simultaneous)
Action takes time போது (During)
Short waiting time வரை / கொஞ்ச நேரம்
Comparing two opposite facts ஆனால் (Contrast)
Situation (Scene) Think in Mind Sentence using while English Meaning
Eating and listening Two actions at the same time While eating, I listen to music Music is listened to during eating
Studying and phone call Long action + interruption While I was studying, my friend called me A call came during study
Sleeping and raining Action during time While I was sleeping, it was raining Rain happened during sleep
Cooking and talking Parallel actions While cooking, she was talking on the phone She talked during cooking
Working and music Same time actions While working, I listen to songs Music is played during work
Walking and accident Time context While walking, I saw an accident Accident was seen while walking
Driving and message Action during another While driving, he received a message Message came during driving
Watching TV and eating Two actions together While watching TV, we ate snacks Snacks were eaten during TV
Waiting for friend Short time wait Wait here for a while Wait for a short time
Sitting quietly Small duration Sit here for a while Sit for some time
Comparing people Contrast He is rich while I am poor Shows contrast
Comparing likes Difference I like tea while she likes coffee Difference in preference
Comparing habits Contrast He works hard while his brother doesn’t Opposite habits
Office situation Opposite states The boss is calm while the staff are nervous Contrast in situation
Life comparison Contrast idea City life is fast while village life is calm Difference between lifestyles

TopicDetails
WordGetting
Tamil Meaningபெறுதல் / கிடைத்துக்கொள்வது / ஆகிக்கொண்டு இருப்பது
Pronunciation (Tamil)கெட்டிங் (get-ting)
Why we use? ஏதாவது ஒன்று கிடைக்குது, நடக்குது, அல்லது ஒரு நிலை மாறிக்கொண்டு இருக்கு nu சொல்ல
Example:
I am getting ideas → எனக்கு ideas கிடைக்குது
Why we use?Example Sentence
ஏதாவது ஒன்று கிடைக்குது (Receiving)I am getting messages.
ஏதாவது நடக்குது (Happening)Things are getting better.
ஒரு நிலை மாறிக்கொண்டு இருக்கு (Change of state)He is getting angry.

Word: Getting – Usage Overview

Context Meaning
Messages, calls, money பெறுதல் (Receiving)
Situation improving / worsening நடக்குது (Happening)
Emotion, health, weather changing நிலை மாறிக்கொண்டு இருக்கு (Change of state)
Skill, habit slowly improving முன்னேறிக்கொண்டு இருக்கு
Situation (Scene) Think in Mind Sentence using getting English Meaning

Office mail Receiving I am getting emails Emails are being received
New ideas Receiving I am getting ideas Ideas are coming to my mind

Anger increasing Change of state He is getting angry His anger is increasing
Feeling tired Change of state I am getting tired Tiredness is increasing
Situation improving Happening Things are getting better Situation is improving
Situation worse Happening Things are getting worse Situation is worsening
Learning English Improvement She is getting better at English Her English skill is improving
Job experience Gradual change He is getting used to the job He is adapting to the job
Noise level Change of state The room is getting noisy Noise is increasing
Age Natural change I am getting older Age is increasing
Confidence Improvement She is getting confident Confidence is growing
Traffic Situation change The road is getting crowded Crowd is increasing

Action / நடப்பது (Happening)I get ready for work every morning → நான் ஒவ்வொரு காலை வேலைக்கு தயார் ஆகிறேன்

Word: Belong

Topic Details
Word Belong
Tamil Meaning சொந்தமானது / சேர்ந்தது / உடையதாக இருக்கிறது
Pronunciation (Tamil) பிலாங் (be-long)
Why we use? Something or someone particular place, group, or person-க்கு உடையதாக இருக்கு nu solla use pannuvom
Example:
This book belongs to me → இந்த புத்தகம் எனக்கு சொந்தமானது
Why we use? Example Sentence
Ownership / சொந்தமானது The keys belong to my father → சாவிகள் என் தந்தைக்கு சொந்தமானவை
Association / சேர்ந்தது She belongs to the dance club → அவள் dance club-க்கு சேர்ந்தவர்
Proper place / உடையதாக இருக்கிறது These tools belong in the garage → இந்த கருவிகள் garahe-ல் இருக்க வேண்டும்

Word: Belong – Usage Overview

Context Meaning
Person owns something சொந்தமானது (Ownership)
Member of group / team சேர்ந்தது (Association)
Correct place for an object உடைய இடம் (Proper place)
Emotional / social fit சேர்ந்திருக்கிறது (Fit in)
Situation (Scene) Think in Mind Sentence using belong English Meaning

Team membership Part of a team He belongs to our team He is in our team
Company staff Association I belong to this company I work for this company
Tools location Correct place These tools belong in the garage Garage is the right place
Shoes location Proper place These shoes belong on the shelf Shelf is the correct place
Files arrangement Proper location These files belong in this folder Folder is the right place
Family connection Emotional fit I belong to this family I am part of this family
Society Social fit He feels he belongs here He feels accepted here
Classroom items Ownership This bag belongs to a student A student owns the bag
Pet ownership Ownership This dog belongs to my neighbor My neighbor owns the dog

Office department Association He belongs to the HR department He works in HR

Rise / உயர்வு Stock prices hiked yesterday → பங்கு விலை நேற்று உயர்ந்தது

Complete / முழுவதும் He read the book through → அவன் புத்தகத்தை முழுவதும் வாசித்தான்
