Discussion Phrases

No Category Phrases
1 Expressing Your Opinion
  • In my view
  • I think that
  • The way I see it
  • I believe that
  • I would say that
2 Agreeing with Someone
  • That’s a solid point, I agree
  • You’ve said exactly what I was thinking
  • Yes, I completely agree with that
3 Disagreeing Politely
  • I see your point, but I have a slightly different view.
  • That’s an interesting point, but we also need to consider…
  • I’d like to offer a different angle on this.
4 Giving Examples or Supporting Your Opinion
  • For example
  • To clarify
  • Consider this example
  • This can be observed in
5 Asking for Clarification
  • Could you explain that in more detail
  • I didn’t quite get that, could you repeat it
  • Could you clarify your point with an example
6 Transitioning to Another Point
  • Let’s now move on to…
  • On a related note,…
  • Now let’s focus on…
7 Seeking Others’ Views
  • What’s your take on this Name
  • I’d love to hear your thoughts on this
  • What do you think about this Name
8 Wrapping Up or Summarizing
  • To summarize…
  • In conclusion…
  • To wrap up, the main takeaway is…
9 Offering a Balanced Opinion
  • On one side, Point A, but on the other, Point B
  • There are pros and cons to both perspectives
  • We can see valid points on both sides
10 Proposing a Solution or Suggestion
  • Maybe we could consider
  • A possible solution could be
  • In my opinion, we should
  • How about we
11 Acknowledging Someone’s Idea
  • That’s a great idea, a N a, me, and I think
  • I like the point Name made about
  • I believe Name raises an important point
12 Acknowledging Another’s Point
  • I see your point and I agree with parts of it
  • You’ve made a solid argument, and it’s something worth considering
  • That’s a valid perspective, and it’s something we should think about
13 Countering an Argument
  • While I understand your point, I think
  • I see where you're coming from, but I believe that
  • That’s a reasonable argument, but there’s another side
14 Respecting Other Viewpoints
  • That’s an interesting point, and it’s something to consider
  • We have different perspectives on this, but
  • I respect your point of view, but I feel that
15 Concluding the Discussion
  • After hearing everyone’s input, I believe
  • Ultimately, we need to focus on
  • To sum up, we can conclude that
